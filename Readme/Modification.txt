1. Rpath package (Rpath_fork)

Compared to the original Rpath, some modifications were made to adapt it to an aquaculture system.

The modifications were made in section R and the following subsections:

- param
- Rpath_methods
- write_functions
- ecopath

Furthermore, the condition  that  the EE values must be less than 1 in the original Rpath  has been removed.





2. EnaR package (enaR_fork)


The FCI formula was changed. In the original implementation in enaR, FCI was calculated as FCI <- TSTc / TST, where TSTc is the total flow cycled and TST is the total system throughflow. It was later modified to FCI <- TSTc / TSTp, where TSTp represents the total system throughput (like in Ecopath model).

These modifications were made in section R and in enaFlow.R.
